Shrek

Shrek (regally known as Sir Shrek) is the titular protagonist of the Shrek franchise. He is Fiona's husband, Donkey and Puss' best friend, the ogre triplets' father, and the son in law of Queen Lillian and King Harold.

  • Main Character
  • Race: Ogre
  • Quote: "What are you doing in my swamp?!"
Per WikiShrek

Donkey

Donkey is the sidekick of the Shrek franchise. He is the best friend and sidekick of Shrek and Fiona along with Puss in Boots, Dragon's husband, and the Dronkeys' father.

  • Main Character
  • Race: Donkey
  • Quote: "Hey, I can fly!"
Per WikiShrek

Fiona

Princess Fiona is one of the main characters in the Shrek franchise and the main female lead. She is the wife of Shrek, the daughter of King Harold and Queen Lillian, and the mother of the Ogre triplets.

  • Main Character
  • Race: Human/Ogre
  • Quote: "Who could ever love a beast so hideous and ugly?"
Per WikiShrek

Lord Farquaad

Lord Farquaad is the ruler of Duloc and the main antagonist of Shrek. He is voiced by John Lithgow.

  • Antagonist
  • Race: Small Human
  • Quote: "Mirror, mirror on the wall / Is this not the most perfect kingdom of them all?"
Per WikiShrek
Puss in Boots

Puss in Boots is Shrek's sidekick. Loosely based on the fairytale character Puss in Boots as well as being a kind of parody of Zorro, he is a smooth-talking cat with a Spanish accent, usually wearing a cavalier's hat, a belt with a sword, a small black cape, and small Corinthian boots.

  • Aiding Character
  • Race: Cat
  • Quote: "Meow" *Spanish Accent*
Per WikiShrek
Prince Charming

Prince Charming is the secondary antagonist of Shrek 2 and the main antagonist of Shrek the Third. He is voiced by Rupert Everett.

  • Antagonist
  • Race: Pretty Human
  • Quote: "I am the rightful King of Far Far Away!"
Per WikiShrek
